About Me

I help creators stop losing viewers.

Most creators struggle not because their ideas are bad — but because their scripts don't hold attention. I fix that.

I write research-based scripts for YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels on topics like psychology, history, business, and self-improvement. My writing is built around one goal: keeping the viewer glued from the first word to the last.

My style is inspired by the storytelling approach of Dhruv Rathee, Mohak Mangal, and Nitish Rajput — clear, conversational, and backed by real research. No fluff. No filler. Just stories that land.
